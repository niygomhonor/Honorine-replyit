About Team Founder

The Neodo Handball Team was created in December 2015, by girl called Neodo Honorine.
After being trained by her friend Esther who was playing handball on National level.
It was great chance to have her as best friend. In words she express her gratefulness,
"She was my best friend, when she was going in warm up, She asked me to go with her, I had good moment in that warm up.
It is the time i had known Jump shoot in handball. In highschool i was a coach of Girls handball team.
So today i have my wide handball team of 200 ladies and 200 gentlemen"
 Said Ms Neodo Honorine
